Our Story

Eagles Citadel Nursery & Primary School

Founded in 2023, Eagles Citadel Nursery and Primary School was born from a vision to create a school where every child — regardless of background — is recognised as a genius waiting to be discovered.

We believe that within every child lies extraordinary potential. Our role as educators and caregivers is to use the right methods, the right environment, and the right heart to unlock it. We combine academic rigour with spiritual grounding, discipline with love, and ambition with affordability.

Mission & Vision

Guided by faith, driven by passion and committed to excellence in everything we do.

Our Mission

Producing pupils who are energetic enough to discover the best of God in them and confidently face challenges — knowing they are built for greatness — through qualitative and affordable education, proper care, discipline and prayer.

🌟

Our Vision

  • To encourage high self-esteem in all children.
  • To produce God-fearing children of strong character.
  • To breed up the gifts and talents in each child.
  • To enlighten children on how to function in this present age.
  • To help maintain extraordinary mental capacity in our pupils.
